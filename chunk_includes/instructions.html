In this experiment you will see strings of letters at the center of the screen.
Your task is to decide whether that string of letters is a word or not in the fastest time possible.


- If you think the string is a word, press 'j' on your keyboard.
- If you think the string is NOT a word, press 'f' on your keyboard.

Before beginning the actual experiment, let's do some practice.
Press 'j' on your keyboard to begin.
